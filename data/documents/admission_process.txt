Admission process overview (undergraduate)

This document explains a typical undergraduate admission workflow:

1) Check eligibility criteria listed in the program brochure.
2) Fill the online application form on the official portal.
3) Upload required documents (ID proof, marksheets, category certificate if applicable).
4) Pay the application fee and submit before the deadline.
5) Track the application status and merit list / counseling schedule.

Important: the admission last date (deadline) can change. Always verify dates on the official notification and portal.
