Required documents for admission

Common documents required during admission or counseling:

- Government ID proof (Aadhaar/passport/driver license)
- Recent photographs
- Marksheets and certificates (10th, 12th, diploma as applicable)
- Transfer certificate / school leaving certificate (if applicable)
- Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) if claiming reservation
- Income certificate if applying for scholarship / fee concession

Keep scanned copies ready for the application form upload step.
