Cutoff and merit list basics

"Cutoff" usually refers to the minimum score/percentage/rank required to be considered for admission.

Merit lists may be formed using:

- Entrance exam score (normalized if multiple sessions)
- Board marks (percentage) or a weighted combination
- Category-wise cutoffs based on seat matrix and reservation rules

If you search for "cutoff", also include "merit list", "seat matrix", and the program name for better retrieval.
