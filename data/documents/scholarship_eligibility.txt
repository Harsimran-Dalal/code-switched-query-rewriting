Scholarship eligibility (common criteria)

Scholarship eligibility varies by scheme, but many programs use a combination of:

- Academic performance: minimum percentage / CGPA in the previous qualifying exam
- Family income threshold (annual household income)
- Category: SC/ST/OBC/EWS or minority categories in some schemes
- Program level: undergraduate vs postgraduate
- Enrollment status: admission confirmed in an eligible institution

Tip: Use keywords like "income certificate", "eligibility", "undergraduate scholarship", and the scheme name to find the right notice.
