FAISS dense retrieval notes

FAISS is a library for efficient similarity search over dense vectors.

Common workflow:
- Encode documents into embeddings (e.g., SentenceTransformers).
- Normalize embeddings and use inner product (IndexFlatIP) to approximate cosine similarity.
- Persist the index to disk, along with metadata mapping index positions to document chunks.

For small academic prototypes, IndexFlatIP is simple and reproducible on CPU.
